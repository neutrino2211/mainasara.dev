# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Primary readers are engineers, security practitioners, and curious builders who come for technical writing and talks. They often read on laptops during focused work sessions, and revisit posts for reference. Secondary readers discover individual posts from social links and need quick orientation without friction.

## Product Purpose

The blog exists to publish thoughtful technical ideas with strong author voice, while staying readable and trustworthy. Success means readers can scan quickly, dive deeply, and leave with a clear takeaway. The interface should make long-form content feel enjoyable, not heavy.

## Brand Personality

Playful, grounded, seasoned. The tone should feel experienced and confident, but never stiff. Personality is shown through typography, rhythm, and subtle interaction details, while content remains the hero.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboards with repetitive card grids
- Overly minimal monochrome blogs that feel sterile
- Neon cyberpunk styling that prioritizes novelty over readability
- Decorative effects that distract from long-form reading

## Design Principles

- Let content lead, let style punctuate.
- Pair mature structure with playful moments.
- Make hierarchy obvious at a glance.
- Preserve calm reading flow across long pages.
- Keep expressive choices intentional and repeatable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and keyboard accessibility for all key interactions. Keep motion subtle and meaningful, avoid motion that blocks reading, and preserve strong readability on mobile with clear type scale and spacing.
